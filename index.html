About the Department

Section: About

The Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) Department at the Western Mindanao State University College of Engineering prepares students to design, build, and sustain electronic and communication systems that power modern society. Our instruction, research, and extension services emphasize both strong theoretical foundations and hands-on learning.

Mission

To cultivate engineers with strong analytical foundations and practical skills to design, build, and maintain electronic and communication systems that uplift communities and industries in Mindanao and beyond.

Vision

To be a leading regional center for education and innovation in communications, signal processing, embedded systems, and automation—recognized for impact-driven research and industry collaboration.

Facilities & Laboratories

Communications Lab

Analog/digital comms, modulation/demodulation, channel coding, SDR experiments.

Electronics Lab

Circuit design and testing, PCB prototyping, power electronics, instrumentation.

Embedded & IoT Lab

Microcontrollers, sensors, networks, rapid prototyping for connected devices.

RF & Antenna Lab

Antenna design, propagation studies, RF front‑end characterization and testing.

Robotics & Control Lab

Mechatronics, motion control, autonomous systems, and industrial automation.

Signal Processing Suite

Audio, image, video analytics, machine learning for intelligent sensing.

Program Outcomes

  • Apply mathematics, science, and engineering principles to complex ECE problems.
  • Design and conduct experiments, analyze and interpret data, and validate models.
  • Design systems and components that meet desired needs with realistic constraints.
  • Use modern engineering and computing tools in professional practice.
  • Communicate effectively in multidisciplinary and diverse teams.
  • Practice ethically and understand societal, environmental, and global impacts.
