About the Department

The Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) Department at the Western Mindanao State University College of Engineering prepares students to design, build, and sustain electronic and communication systems that power modern society. Our instruction, research, and extension services emphasize both strong theoretical foundations and hands-on learning.
